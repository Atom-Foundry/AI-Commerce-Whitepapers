# AI Readability™

## Abstract

The first challenge in AI commerce is not trust.

It is not authority.

It is not recommendation.

It is readability.

Before an AI system can understand a business, it must first be able to read it.

AI Readability™ is the discipline of measuring how effectively AI systems can access, parse, extract, and interpret commercial information.

As commerce shifts toward AI-assisted discovery and recommendation, machine readability becomes foundational infrastructure.

---

## The Human Assumption

Most websites were built for people.

Navigation menus, visual hierarchy, banners, product grids, and landing pages were designed around human browsing behavior.

AI systems interact differently.

They do not browse.

They retrieve information.

They extract relationships.

They build understanding.

This creates a new optimization challenge.

---

## What Is AI Readability™

AI Readability™ measures how easily AI systems can access and process information.

The framework seeks to answer:

**Can AI successfully read and interpret this content?**

The question is not whether content exists.

The question is whether machines can effectively use it.

---

## Why Readability Matters

Every downstream AI capability depends on readability.

Without readability there is:

* No extraction
* No understanding
* No trust
* No recommendation

AI Readability™ acts as the first stage of AI Commerce Intelligence™.

---

## Components Of AI Readability™

### Structured Information

Information should be organized consistently.

### Machine Readability

Important content should be accessible to AI systems.

### Semantic Clarity

Meaning should be easy to interpret.

### Product Completeness

Products should contain sufficient information for evaluation.

### Entity Identification

Brands, products, and merchants should be clearly defined.

---

## Common Readability Problems

Many businesses unintentionally create barriers.

Examples include:

* Missing product information
* Weak schema implementation
* Inconsistent terminology
* Information hidden inside images
* Fragmented content structures

These issues reduce machine understanding.

---

## The Readability Advantage

As AI systems become recommendation engines, readability becomes a competitive advantage.

Businesses with higher AI Readability™ may benefit from:

* Better understanding
* Stronger trust signals
* Increased recommendation eligibility
* Greater recommendation consistency

---

## Readability And Recommendation

Recommendation is impossible without interpretation.

Interpretation is impossible without readability.

The recommendation economy begins with machine-readable commerce.

---

## Conclusion

Search visibility helped businesses become discoverable.

AI Readability™ helps businesses become understandable.

As AI increasingly participates in commercial decision making, readability may become one of the most important foundations of digital commerce.
